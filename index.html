About KITH Mineral Water

KITH Mineral Water is a trusted provider of clean, safe, and refreshing drinking water. Our water goes through advanced purification systems to ensure high quality for our consumers. We are committed to health, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Water Processing Stages

1. Source Collection

Water is sourced from a deep underground borehole to ensure natural purity.

2. Pre-Filtration

Removes large particles and debris through sand and carbon filters.

3. Reverse Osmosis

Advanced RO system removes dissolved salts, bacteria, and contaminants.

4. UV Sterilization

UV light eliminates any remaining microorganisms ensuring complete disinfection.

5. Ozonation

Ozone gas is added to maintain freshness and increase shelf life.

6. Mineral Addition

Essential minerals are added back for health and taste benefits.

7. Bottling & Packaging

Water is bottled in hygienic conditions and sealed with quality checks.

8. Distribution

Final products are distributed across retail stores, wholesalers, and home delivery routes.
